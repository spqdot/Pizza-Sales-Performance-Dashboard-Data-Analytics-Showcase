
🍕 Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard | Data Analytics Showcase
📌 Project Overview

The Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard is a business intelligence project developed to analyze sales performance, product trends, and customer purchasing behavior.

Built using Microsoft Power BI, this dashboard transforms raw transactional sales data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making in marketing, operations, pricing, and inventory management.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how data visualization and analytical modeling can uncover revenue drivers and performance patterns within a retail food business environment.

🎯 Business Objectives

This dashboard was designed to answer key business questions:

Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?

What size and category combinations drive the most sales?

Are there seasonal or monthly sales trends?

How does quantity sold vary by product size?

What insights can support inventory and pricing optimization?

📊 Key Insights
1️⃣ Top-Performing Products

Thai Chicken Pizza and Barbecue Chicken Pizza are the leading products by total sales.

High-performing specialty pizzas significantly contribute to overall revenue.

2️⃣ Sales Breakdown by Category & Size

Large and Medium pizzas dominate revenue contribution.

Category-level analysis reveals clear customer preferences across product segments.

3️⃣ Monthly Sales Trends

Time-series analysis highlights seasonal fluctuations.

Identifies peak performance months to support demand forecasting and promotional planning.

4️⃣ Quantity Analysis by Size

Large pizzas lead in total revenue.

Quantity distribution supports strategic inventory planning and cost management decisions.
